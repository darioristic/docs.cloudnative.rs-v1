# Onboarding Guide

This page provides a comprehensive guide for onboarding new sales team members.

## New Team Member Checklist

- Complete technical training modules
- Review company capabilities and positioning
- Study client engagement processes
- Familiarize with tools and resources

## Training Modules

- Cloud-native fundamentals
- Our platform capabilities
- Client engagement best practices
- Technical assessment processes

## Resources and Support

- Mentorship program details
- Internal training materials
- External certification paths
- Regular team knowledge sharing sessions
