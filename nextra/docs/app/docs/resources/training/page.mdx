# Training & Certification

This page provides comprehensive training resources and certification programs for developing expertise in OpenShift and cloud-native technologies. Our training programs are designed to support teams at all skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

## Training Overview

### Learning Philosophy

Our training approach emphasizes:

- **Hands-on Experience**: Practical labs and real-world scenarios
- **Progressive Learning**: Structured learning paths from basic to advanced
- **Industry Standards**: Alignment with industry best practices and certifications
- **Continuous Learning**: Ongoing education and skill development programs

### Target Audiences

#### Developers
- Application developers transitioning to cloud-native

- API development and integration

#### Platform Engineers

#### Operations Teams

- Customer enablement and support

## Certification Programs

### Certification Levels

#### Foundation Level
- **Target**: Entry-level practitioners
- **Requirements**: Complete fundamentals training
- **Exam Format**: Multiple choice and hands-on labs
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Renewal**: Continuing education credits

#### Professional Level
- **Target**: Experienced practitioners
- **Requirements**: Foundation certification + experience
- **Exam Format**: Scenario-based and practical exercises
- **Validity**: 3 years
- **Renewal**: Advanced training or recertification

#### Expert Level
- **Target**: Senior practitioners and architects
- **Requirements**: Professional certification + advanced training
- **Exam Format**: Design and implementation scenarios
- **Validity**: 3 years
- **Renewal**: Expert-level training or contribution

### Certification Tracks

#### Platform Track
- OpenShift Fundamentals → Administrator → Advanced Administrator
- Focus on platform management and operations
- Target audience: Platform engineers and administrators

#### Development Track
- Container Fundamentals → OpenShift Developer → Advanced Developer
- Focus on application development and deployment
- Target audience: Developers and DevOps engineers

#### Security Track
- Security Fundamentals → Container Security → Compliance Specialist
- Focus on security implementation and compliance
- Target audience: Security engineers and compliance officers

#### Operations Track
- Observability Fundamentals → Incident Response → Performance Specialist
- Focus on operational excellence and reliability
- Target audience: SREs and operations engineers
